# 🗿 Rusty Golem

**Smart Minecraft bots, built for people.**

We're building fast, reliable, AI-native bots that can live inside a Minecraft world — watching,
thinking, and acting all on their own. Our goal is to turn that technology into simple products
anyone can use.

## How a Golem works

Each bot follows one simple loop:

```
   👀  See      →      🤔  Think      →      🛠️  Act
the world             what to do            in the game
```

It sees what's happening around it, decides what to do, and takes action — then does it all over
again, many times a second.

## How it all fits together

We're building from the ground up, in three layers:

```
   🎮  Products people use
       companions · helpers · guardians · live streams
                    ▲
   🧠  The Golem brain
       sees the world · makes decisions · takes action
                    ▲
   🧱  A rock-solid foundation
       fast, reliable building blocks, each doing one job well
```

Every Golem stands on a carefully engineered foundation of small, self-contained pieces. On top
of that sits its "brain." And on top of that are the products people actually get to use.

## Our vision

Today, putting a smart bot into a game takes real technical skill. We don't think it should.

We're building the engine now — carefully, piece by piece — so that one day anyone can have their
own Golem: a tireless companion that explores, builds, guards, and plays right alongside them.

**Fast. Efficient. AI-native. Built for people.**

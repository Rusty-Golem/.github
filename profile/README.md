## How a Golem works

Each bot follows one simple loop — many times a second:

<p align="center">
  <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Rusty-Golem/.github/main/profile/assets/loop.svg" alt="See, then Think, then Act — and repeat" width="780">
</p>

It sees what's happening around it, decides what to do, and takes action — then does it all over again.

## How it all fits together

We build from the ground up, in three layers:

<p align="center">
  <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Rusty-Golem/.github/main/profile/assets/stack.svg" alt="A foundation of building blocks, the Golem brain, and the products people use" width="760">

Every Golem stands on a carefully engineered foundation of small, self-contained pieces. On top of
that sits its "brain." And on top of that are the products people actually get to use.

## Our vision

Today, putting a smart bot into a game takes real technical skill. We don't think it should.

We're building the engine now — carefully, piece by piece — so that one day anyone can have their
own Golem: a tireless companion that explores, builds, guards, and plays right alongside them.
